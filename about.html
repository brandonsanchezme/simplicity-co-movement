My Story

From transformation
to movement.

A few years ago, I lost 55 pounds and with it, I gained confidence I never knew I had. That transformation changed my life and inspired me to help other women feel the same strength, confidence, and empowerment within their own bodies.

SimplicityCo Movement was born out of a simple belief: fitness is better together. I wanted to create experiences that made women fall in love with movement — not dread it. Events that felt elevated, intentional, and genuinely fun.

Through events, training, workshops, and collaboration, my mission is simple: to help women feel strong, connected, and confident — in their bodies, their businesses, and their lives.

Together, we're building something bigger. A movement rooted in empowerment, community, and intention.

Our Mission
