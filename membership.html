Got questions?

What exactly do I get for $10/month?+
You get access to our private community group chat with daily check-ins, business insights, exclusive workout content, discounted event tickets, discounted 1:1 training, and a network of like-minded women entrepreneurs.
How much are event discounts for members?+
Members receive exclusive discounted pricing on all public events plus early access before tickets open to the public. Members-only events are also available at no additional cost.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes — membership is month-to-month and you can cancel at any time. There are no long-term commitments.
Do I need to be in Arizona?+
The community group chat, business content, and workout content are all virtual and available anywhere. In-person events are currently hosted in the Arizona area.
Is this just for fitness people?+
Not at all! This community is for any woman who wants to grow — personally, professionally, and physically. Whether you're into fitness or just looking for a community of motivated women, you're welcome here.
